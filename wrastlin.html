Wrastlin' was a mini-series that me and my life long associate Seamus O'Donnel sketched out while he was visiting me in Halle in the Summer of 2013. It draws inspiration from an ensemble of characters we created originally using the WWE 2K13 'custom superstar' feature for the PlayStation 3.

They continue to live on through the lore we continually renew, the inside jokes, caricatures, and even occassional performance re-enactment. This work earns a place on the site as it is some of the most sentimental and authentic material we have ever created.

First Event: Le Monke and L'Ape versus Old Man and The Undertaker

A depiction of a tag team match between two custom characters from our youth. The Undertaker is already a well known athlete in sports entertainment, however his partner may not be so recognizable, as he was modelled on a figure from our local community. Le Monke and L'Ape are two apes, L'Ape being the larger of the two. They were notoriously hard to defeat."

Newcomer Shetlan versus Stenchy

Shetlan demonstrates real commitment to the craft and testify's to his supremacy as a wrestler by performing a 'top rope move'. Meanwhile lying semi-conscious center of the ring is Stenchy, who was inspired by a deflated balloon animal that originally took the form of a morbid found object. Shetlan is the name of a budget brand of detergent.
